About us

Our story

Small in size. Big on focus.

Immigration Consulting was created with one simple idea: By focusing on fewer, well-defined tasks, we can deliver better, faster and safer processes. We are a small, specialized unit – and that is a deliberate choice.

Why small and targeted?

We would rather say no to a case than compromise on quality. By being selective, we can immerse ourselves, know the details of your specific case – and take responsibility for a process that feels thorough from start to finish.

Experience from the other side

Our advisors and consultants have many years of authority-near experience – e.g. from SIRI. We know the processes, documentation requirements and pitfalls inside out, because we have worked with the cases on the other side.

Guidelines

How we ensure a safe and correct process

1) Scope of advice

  • We are advisors/consultants. We do not make decisions and are not an authority.
  • We fill in and submit applications (e.g. AR1/AR6 and AR3/AR7) and coordinate dialogue – biometrics and personal appearance are handled at SIRI.
  • We can submit with power of attorney, where relevant.

2) Authority requirements & biometrics

  • Non-EU/EEA applicants must have biometric data recorded at application/extension; appointments are booked at SIRI.
  • Authority fees are paid directly to SIRI by the applicant/employer.

3) New applications (AR1/AR6)

  • We quality assure contracts, salaries and conditions under the relevant scheme and submit correct material from the start.
  • With power of attorney, the employer/third party can submit on behalf of the applicant.

4) Extensions (AR3/AR7)

  • Conditions must still be met (e.g. same employer/position, correct salary).
  • We recommend starting well before expiry to avoid interruption.

5) Documentation & duty of truth

  • The client is responsible for the accuracy of all information and documents.
  • We will inform you if further documentation/translation is necessary.

6) Data protection (GDPR)

  • We only process necessary information on a legal basis and practice data minimization and access control.
  • Deletion/archiving according to clear deadlines. Agreements with data processors and appropriate security level.

7) Prices & responsibility

  • Prices are communicated clearly before startup. Authority fees are separate.
  • We cannot guarantee outcomes or processing times (decisions are made by SIRI).

8) Ethics & conflicts of interest

  • We do not take on cases with obvious conflicts of interest and refer if necessary to independent advice.
  • We work fairly and respectfully towards applicants, companies and authorities.

Note: Rules and practices are continuously updated. We always advise according to the applicable guidelines at nyidanmark.dk/SIRI.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we start?

Typically the same business day, once we have your contact details and case data.

What do we need to send you?

Job offer/contract, company and employee contact details, and relevant documents (passport, etc.). We will provide a checklist.

Do you handle biometrics or authority fees?

No – as advisors we handle the application, documentation and follow-up. Biometrics/fees are handled at SIRI.

Can we get a meeting?

Yes. Scheduled advice costs 987 DKK excl. VAT per hour. Many get started well via a free initial clarification through the form.
