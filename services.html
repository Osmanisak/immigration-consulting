Services

We handle both new applications and extensions – from document checks to submission and dialogue with SIRI.

New applications AR1/AR6

We set the case up correctly from the start to avoid errors and delays.

  • Contract and salary requirement checks
  • Completion and submission of AR1/AR6
  • Checklists and document collection
  • Dialogue and follow-up with SIRI
  • Status updates until decision

Extensions AR3/AR7

Confident extension of existing residence and work permits.

  • Review of conditions, changes and deadlines
  • Completion and submission of AR3/AR7
  • Updated documents and requirements
  • Coordination and responses to SIRI
  • Status updates until decision
